Artificial Intelligence

Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a technology that allows computers and machines to think and learn like humans. It helps systems analyze data, recognize patterns, and make decisions automatically.

How It Is Used

AI is used in many applications such as voice assistants, recommendation systems, self-driving cars, and chatbots. It helps make work faster and improves efficiency in many industries.

Conclusion

Artificial Intelligence is becoming an important part of modern technology. It helps solve complex problems and makes many everyday tasks easier.
